The Magic Behind the Scenes

A state-of-the-art AI pipeline powers every video.

1. Idea & Locations

Concepts and locations flow through a predefined list on Google Sheets, approved by a human through Telegram, managed entirely by n8n workflows.

2. Scripts & Captions

Gemini, Claude, and ChatGPT craft the perfect script, social captions, and @ mentions.

3. Photos & Video

Photos by Nano Banana 2 and video by Veo3.1 through fal.ai, voice by Elevenlabs. Everything is stitched together automagically.

4. Publication

The final masterpiece and copy are published to all social platforms automatically via Blotato.
